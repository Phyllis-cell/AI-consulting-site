Which models & clouds do you support?

How do you measure ROI?

We define a north‑star metric in week 1 (e.g., review time saved, CSAT, conversion lift) and track it with an evaluation harness plus business KPIs.

Data privacy & compliance?

PII is masked or isolated; we support HIPAA/PCI workflows and can run within your VPC. Source code & weights remain your IP.

Yes—playbooks, enablement sessions and hands‑on pairing are included with every engagement.
